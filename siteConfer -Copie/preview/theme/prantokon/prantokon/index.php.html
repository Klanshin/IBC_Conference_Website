Foreword

Dear collegues,

The International Inflammatory Breast Cancer (IBC) consortium organises the 1st Workshop on Inflammatory Breast Cancer.

Our primary goal is to spread worldwide knowledge about IBC. We organize educational conferences and workshops that are evidence

-based and updated to latest advancement in IBC management. We believe that strong collaboration between health professionals and

advocates is essential to raise awareness and face the challenges faced by this under-represented group of patients.

It is therefore with great enthusiasm that we invite you to attend the 1st workshop that will take place in Tunis, Tunisia, On

September 25th-26th, 2019. Toward a better IBC patients care.



Pr. Hamouda BOUSSEN & Pr. Massimo CRISTOFANILLI
